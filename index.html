Welcome to Swiggy

Swiggy is one of India’s largest online food delivery platforms that connects customers with restaurants and delivers food directly to their homes. It was founded in 2014 by Sriharsha Majety, Nandan Reddy, and Rahul Jaimini. The company operates in hundreds of cities across India and provides fast delivery services through its large network of delivery partners. Besides food delivery, Swiggy also offers services like Instamart for groceries and Genie for parcel delivery. Its headquarters is located in Bengaluru.
